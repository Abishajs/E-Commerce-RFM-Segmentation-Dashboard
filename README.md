# E-Commerce Customer Behavior Analysis & RFM Segmentation Dashboard

## Project Overview

Developed an end-to-end Power BI Business Intelligence solution to analyze customer behavior, revenue performance, product sales, and customer segmentation using the RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) framework.

The dashboard helps identify high-value customers, monitor sales performance, evaluate product profitability, and generate actionable business recommendations for customer retention and revenue growth.

## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- MySQL
- DAX
- Power Query
- Excel

## Dataset Overview

- 5,878 Customers Analyzed
- 37,000+ Orders Processed
- £17.79M Total Revenue Evaluated
- Data Period: Dec 2009 – Dec 2011

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Overview

Provides a high-level business performance summary including:

- Total Customers
- Total Orders
- Total Revenue
- Average Order Value
- Monthly Revenue Trend Analysis
- Peak Revenue Month Identification
- Lowest Revenue Month Analysis
- Seasonal Revenue Insights
- Revenue by Country
- Top Revenue-Contributing Customers
- Geographic Revenue Concentration Analysis

### Key Insights

- United Kingdom contributes over 80% of total revenue.
- Revenue consistently peaks during Q4 (October–December).
- November recorded the highest revenue period.
- Revenue shows strong seasonal buying behavior.

---

### 2. Customer & RFM Segmentation Analysis

Implemented RFM Segmentation to classify customers based on:

- Recency
- Frequency
- Monetary Value

### Customer Segments

- Champions
- Loyal Customers
- Potential Customers
- New Customers
- At Risk Customers
- Lost Customers

### Features

- Revenue Contribution by Segment
- Segment Distribution Analysis
- Average Customer Spend by Segment
- Customer Detail View
- Interactive Business Action Center
- Dynamic Manager Recommendation Panel

### Business Actions Generated

- Reward Champions with VIP benefits
- Upsell Loyal Customers
- Engage Potential Customers
- Retain At-Risk Customers
- Win Back Lost Customers

---

### 3. Product Performance Analysis

Analyzes product-level performance and inventory priorities.

### Features

- Top Products by Revenue
- Top Products by Quantity Sold
- Product Strategy Matrix
- Product Prioritization Framework
- Strategic Business Recommendation Panel

### Strategic Recommendations

- Maintain stock availability for top-selling products
- Increase marketing efforts before Q4 peak season
- Reward high-value customers
- Launch retention campaigns for At-Risk customers
- Win back Lost Customers through targeted offers

---

## Key Business Insights

### Customer Insights

- Champions generate the highest revenue contribution.
- Loyal Customers represent a strong retention opportunity.
- Lost and At-Risk customers require immediate engagement strategies.

### Revenue Insights

- Strong revenue dependence on the UK market.
- Significant seasonal demand spikes during Q4.
- Revenue concentration highlights expansion opportunities in other countries.

### Product Insights

- A small group of products contributes a large share of total revenue.
- Inventory planning before peak seasons is critical.
- Product bundling and cross-selling opportunities exist.
